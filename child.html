Fun Activities for Children - Cybersecurity Education

Cybersecurity Scavenger Hunt

Create a list of cybersecurity terms and hide clues around the house or classroom. As children find each term, discuss its meaning and importance for staying safe online.

Password Creation Challenge

Challenge children to come up with creative and secure passwords. Provide a list of common words or phrases and encourage them to turn them into strong passwords.

Phishing Email Role-Play

Divide children into groups and assign each group a scenario involving a phishing email. Have them act out how they would respond to the email and discuss strategies for staying safe online.

Cybersecurity Comics

Have children create their own comic strips or storyboards that illustrate common cybersecurity scenarios. Encourage them to use characters and dialogue to demonstrate how to recognize and respond to potential threats.

Online Safety Board Game

Design a board game that teaches children about online safety practices. Include spaces for different scenarios, such as encountering a pop-up ad or receiving a friend request from a stranger.

Internet Safety Puzzles

Create puzzles or crossword puzzles featuring cybersecurity terms and concepts. Children can work individually or in groups to solve the puzzles, helping them familiarize themselves with important cybersecurity vocabulary.

Cybersecurity Kahoot Quiz

Use the Kahoot platform to create an interactive quiz about cybersecurity. Include questions about common online threats, safety tips, and how to protect personal information.
